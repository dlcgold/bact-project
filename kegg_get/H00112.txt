ENTRY       H00112                      Disease
NAME        Paratyphoid fever
DESCRIPTION Paratyphoid fever is a systemic febrile illness endemic in developing countries where sanitation is lacking and access to clean water is reduced. The disease is similar to typhoid fever [DS:H00111] but gives a milder infection.
