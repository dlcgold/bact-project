ENTRY       H01041                      Disease
NAME        Aerococcus urinae infection
DESCRIPTION Aerococcus urinae is a rare pathogen that belongs to a gram-positive coccus. Most infections are mild, but fatal infections such as endocarditis and septicemia/urosepsis have also been described.
