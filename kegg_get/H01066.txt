ENTRY       H01066                      Disease
NAME        Bordetella petrii infection
DESCRIPTION Bordetella petrii is one of the members of the genus Bordetella, that includes human pathogen B. pertussis. Unlike other members of Bordetella, B. petrii is an environmental species but sometimes can infect immunocompetent humans.
