ENTRY       H01077                      Disease
NAME        Bordetella hinzii infection
DESCRIPTION Bordetella hinzii is a recently added species to the genus Bordetella that is isolated from poultry with respiratory disease. B. hinzii is a potential human pathogen in immunocompromised patients or patients with underlying disease.
