ENTRY       H01081                      Disease
NAME        Achromobacter xylosoxidans keratitis
DESCRIPTION Achromobacter xylosoxidans is a gram-negative bacterium that belongs to the genus Alicaligenes. This organism can rarely cause keratitis in patients who had worn contact lenses.
CATEGORY    Infectious disease

COMMENT     Achromobacter xylosoxidans is resistant to aminoglycosides.
