ENTRY       H01147                      Disease
NAME        Methylobacterium infection
DESCRIPTION Methylobacterium species are fastidious, pink-pigmented, gram-negative bacilli that rarely cause human infections. It has been reported to that the bacterium can cause catheter-related infection in both immunocompromised and immunocompetent patients.
