ENTRY       H01148                      Disease
NAME        Caulobacter infection
DESCRIPTION The genus Caulobacter is a gram-negative bacteria characterized by asymmetric cell division and stalk. Although infection with Caulobacter species is rare, a case has been reported in a patient undergoing intermittent peritoneal dialysis.
CATEGORY    Infectious disease
BRITE       Infectious diseases [BR:br08401]
             Bacterial infections
              Infections caused by alpha proteobacteria
               H01148  Caulobacter infection
PATHOGEN    Caulobacter sp.
COMMENT     The isolate in the first report was found to be sensitive to meropenem, imipenem, erythromycin (disk only), aminoglycosides, tetracycline, chloramphenicol, and sulfadiazine.
