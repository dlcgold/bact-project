ENTRY       H01151                      Disease
NAME        Brevundimonas infection
DESCRIPTION Brevundimonas species are aerobic non-fermenting gram-negative bacilli. They are infrequently isolated from clinical samples and can cause opportunistic infections in patients with underlying diseases.
