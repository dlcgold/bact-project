ENTRY       H01166                      Disease
NAME        Sphingomonas paucimobilis infection
DESCRIPTION Sphingomonas paucimobilis is a yellow-pigmented, non-fermentative, gram-negative bacillus found in water. This bacterium has  been reported to cause nosocomial infections and is considered to originate from contaminated hospital environment and equipment.
