ENTRY       H01517                      Disease
NAME        Erysipelas
  SUPERGRP  Streptococcal infection [DS:H00333]
DESCRIPTION Streptococcus pyogenes (group A Streptococcus) is the usual etiologic agent of erysipelas. Symptoms and signs of erysipelas are chills, fever, a well-demarcated, erythematous, indurated, rapidly spreading patch with a palpable advancing border on the face or extremities. This disease most frequently affects infants, small children and the elderly.
