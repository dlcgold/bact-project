ENTRY       H02029                      Disease
NAME        Mycobacterium avium complex (MAC) pulmonary disease
  SUPERGRP  Nontuberculous mycobacterial infection [DS:H01458]
DESCRIPTION Nontuberculous mycobacteria (NTM), including Mycobacterium avium complex (MAC), cause opportunistic chronic pulmonary infections. MAC includes M. avium, M. intracellulare, and eight other species, including M. chimaera. MAC pulmonary disease has been described with two types of clinical manifestations: fibrocavitary and nodular bronchiectatic.
